Service Descriptions

Classic Eyelashes 

Perfect for enhancing your natural lashes with a subtle, elegant look. Our Classic Eyelash Extensions add length and a touch of volume to your lashes. This timeless style is ideal for those who desire a more natural, everyday look with a polished finish.   

What's included: 

  • Individual synthetic lashes applied to your natural lashes.
  • Light and comfortable for all-day wear.
  • Designed to mimic the natural lash line.

    • Best for: 

    A natural and effortless enhancement.

    Hybrid Eyelashes 

    For those who want the best of both worlds – a blend of classic and volume lashes for more drama. Our Hybrid Extensions combine the simplicity of classic lashes with the fullness of volume lashes. This look adds more definition and depth to your eyes, without being too heavy.    

    What's included: 

  • A mix of classic lashes and volume fans for added fullness.
  • A customizable blend depending on your desired look.
  • o Perfect for enhancing your natural beauty with extra flair.

    • Best for: 

    A balance between natural and voluminous.

    Volume Eyelashes 

    For a full, dramatic look that makes a statement. Volume Eyelash Extensions are designed for maximum volume and intensity. Perfect for those looking for thick, bold lashes that make an impression. Using lighter, thinner lashes, we create fuller fans for a dramatic yet lightweight finish.   

    What's included: 

  • Multiple ultra-fine lashes applied to each natural lash.
  • Creates a fuller, more voluminous appearance.
  • Ideal for special occasions or for those who love a bold lash look.

    • Best for: 

    A bold and lightweight look.

    Mega Volume 

    Go all out with the most dramatic, ultra-voluminous look! Mega Volume lashes use extra-fine, ultra-lightweight fans to create the fullest, most luxurious lash effect possible. Ideal for special occasions or those who love dramatic beauty.   

    What's included: 

  • Mega volume sets use 0.05 and 0.03 lash extensions to create a fan with up to 16 lash extensions.
  • This service is recommended for someone who is looking to have a super dramatic lash look
  • Ideal to emphasize your eyes in photographs for weddings, proms, and special events.

    • Best for: 

    An iconic, striking, and ultra-glamorous statement.
